关于 ArxivPulse

🎯 本网站专为 RESET 小组打造，用于组内学术交流与知识分享 This website is designed for RESET group for academic exchange and knowledge sharing

ArxivPulse 是一个面向 RESET 小组的学术文章检索与展示平台，致力于帮助团队成员高效追踪 Software Engineering 领域的最新研究进展。平台每日自动从 arXiv 获取最新论文，通过智能关键词匹配和标签系统，将高质量的研究成果精准推送给需要的同学。我们相信，及时的学术资讯能够激发创新灵感，促进团队内部的知识交流与协作。

ArxivPulse is an academic paper search and display platform for the RESET group, dedicated to helping team members efficiently track the latest research progress in Software Engineering. The platform automatically fetches the latest papers from arXiv daily and uses intelligent keyword matching and tag systems to deliver high-quality research to team members. We believe that timely academic information can inspire innovation and promote knowledge exchange and collaboration within the team.

核心功能

  • 🔄 自动同步：每日自动获取 arxiv 最新论文
  • 🏷️ 标签筛选：支持多标签分类和筛选
  • 🔍 全文搜索：支持标题、作者、摘要搜索
  • 📱 响应式设计：支持桌面和移动端
  • 📬 消息推送：飞书定时推送整理后的文章
  • ✅ 标签扩展：支持动态添加新标签
